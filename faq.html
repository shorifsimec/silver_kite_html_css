Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Silver Kite Event Management Services.

1. What types of events do you manage?

We manage corporate events, weddings, brand activations, product launches, seminars, conferences, and private parties.

2. How can I book your service?

You can book our services by filling out the contact form on our website or calling us directly via phone or WhatsApp.

3. How early should I book an event?

We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance. For large-scale events, earlier booking ensures better planning and availability.

4. Do you offer customized event packages?

Yes, we provide fully customized packages based on your budget, theme, and requirements.

5. Do you handle venue and decoration?

Yes, we manage venue selection, decoration, lighting, sound systems, and full event setup.

6. How is the event cost calculated?

Costs depend on event type, guest count, location, design requirements, and additional services.

7. Is advance payment required?

Yes, an advance payment is required to confirm your booking and begin planning.

8. What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms depend on the project stage. Partial refunds may apply if cancelled within an agreed timeframe.

9. Do you provide on-site support during events?

Yes, our professional team remains present on-site to manage and ensure smooth execution of the event.
